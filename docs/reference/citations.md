## Examples of APA Format

Book:
Smith, J. D. (2023). *Introduction to robotics*. Academic Press.

Journal Article:
Johnson, A. B., & Williams, C. D. (2022). Advances in humanoid robotics. *Journal of Robotics*, 15(3), 45-67.

Conference Paper:
Lee, S., & Chen, M. (2023). Physical AI for humanoid systems. In *Proceedings of the International Conference on Robotics and Automation* (pp. 123-130).

Website:
NVIDIA Corporation. (2023). Isaac Sim documentation. https://docs.nvidia.com/isaacsim/

## Adding New Citations

When adding new content to the textbook, ensure all claims are backed by verified sources from:
- Robotics journals
- AI conference papers
- Official documentation (ROS, NVIDIA, Unity, etc.)
- Peer-reviewed publications

All citations must be formatted according to APA 7th edition guidelines.
