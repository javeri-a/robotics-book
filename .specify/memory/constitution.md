# Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics Textbook Constitution

## Core Principles

### Fact Verification
Verify all facts with strong sources.

### Clear Communication
Keep writing simple and direct.

### Proper Citations
Use APA citations.

### Originality
Maintain full originality.

### Reproducibility
Keep all methods reproducible.

### Technical Accuracy
Ensure technical accuracy in all content.

## Scope and Technology Stack
Cover Physical AI foundations, ROS 2 control systems, Gazebo and Unity simulations, NVIDIA Isaac for perception and training, Vision language action systems, and Humanoid locomotion and interaction.

## Educational Goals
Give students practical control pipelines, connect simulation to real hardware, teach core skills for humanoid robotics, support a full voice driven humanoid capstone.

## Governance
Constitution serves as the authoritative guide for all project decisions; All content must comply with the principles outlined; Regular reviews ensure continued adherence.
